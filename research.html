INTRODUCTION

This field in business is known as PRICE OPTIMISATION AND MANAGEMENT. It is a sub field of sales and supply chain planning. Especially large businesses who have operations globally and thousands of SKUs (Stock Keeping Unit also known as products) need to strategise and execute product pricing to ensure profitability while also being competitive in their key markets.

This is true for both B2C and B2B business. Price optimisation is a multivariate analysis where analysts must consider facts such as competitor price data, historically pricing, localized regional pricing, distributor/dealer discounting and a host of other factors such as shipping rates and local taxes.

Additionally, as customer continues to place orders, the deal history is also important to make decisions on strategic customer pricing. Profitability is also key as product costs can vary based on internal factors and also external factors such as the upward or downward movement of commodity raw material prices globally like oil, gold, rubber etc. For large businesses with thousands of SKUs doing this activity efficiently is not possible using simple tools such as Excel sheets. Therefore, the enterprise software vendors have a number of solutions offerings to management and optimise the pricing process.

METHODOLOGY

The main objective of this proposed system is to predict and optimize the best price for future products and also to provide an efficient and trustworthy solution to replace the existing manual processes. The final web platform will consist of the following components:

  1. Competitor component predicts future price of competitors.
  2. Cost prediction component which predicts the cost of future production including material cost, labour cost, overhead cost etc.
  3. Shipping and Exchange rate Component that predicts future exchange rate and shipping rates.
  4. A component which analyze customer behaviors by categorizing customer groups and calculating customerâ€™s value
  5. An optimization module which optimizes the price and maximize profit.

Users of this system will access this platform using a web application interface which is connected to a RESTful API and a database. The core component of this system is optimization module which is going to serve optimized price value to the web platform through a Restful API .The final output of above four components will then be fed to the optimization module for further processing through a RESTful API.
